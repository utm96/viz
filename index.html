Impact of Generative AI on Jobs

Research question: What portion of tasks in a given job role are automated and which ones still require human intervention due to the integration of generative AI models in different industries?

Viz title: Job Vision

Source: Code is customized from p5's example (source)

Description:

Each rectangle in this visualization represents a unique domain. The size of the rectangle is based on the total of tasks in that domain. Within each domain, various job roles are depicted, and the impact of AI on each domain is illustrated through color coding. The shade of gray corresponds to the level of AI impact in percentage terms. A lighter shade indicates a lesser impact of AI on that domain, while a darker shade signifies a higher impact.
